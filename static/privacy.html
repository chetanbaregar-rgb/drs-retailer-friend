Privacy & Your Data

How RetEarn Ready handles information when you chat with us

Who we are

What this notice covers

This notice explains, in plain English, what personal data we collect when you use the RetEarn Ready chat, why we collect it, the legal basis for doing so, how long we keep it, and the rights you have under the UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018.

What we collect — and only with your agreement

You can use the chat without sharing anything about yourself. The DRS guidance never depends on it. Only if you agree ("Yes, tailor it to me" on the welcome banner) do we record a small picture of your business so our answers fit your situation:

  • Your general location (town/region and UK nation)
  • The type of store you run
  • The rough size of your shop floor
  • How many stores you operate
  • Your role (e.g. owner, manager)
  • Why you're looking into DRS

If you agree, we also keep a copy of the chat itself so the conversation can pick up where it left off.

At the natural end of a conversation, the assistant may offer to keep you posted on DRS updates (registration windows, grant dates, etc.). If you choose to share your name and email address at that point, we'll store those alongside your business profile so we can send you relevant updates. This is entirely optional — the advice you receive is the same either way.

Data minimisation. We keep location general (town or region, never a full address or postcode). Contact details are only collected if you choose to share them. We do not ask for a phone number.

Our lawful basis

Where you agree to the above, our lawful basis is your consent (UK GDPR Article 6(1)(a)). You gave it freely, you can withdraw it at any time, and withdrawing it is as easy as giving it — see "Your choices and rights" below. If you decline, we process nothing beyond what is strictly necessary to show you the page.

Cookies and local storage

We don't use advertising or tracking cookies. To make the chat work, your browser stores a small random session reference locally on your device. If you decline data collection, that reference is temporary and is not linked to any stored profile. This is a strictly-necessary function under the Privacy and Electronic Communications Regulations (PECR), so it does not require separate consent.

Who can see your data

  • Our AI provider (Anthropic). Your messages are sent to Anthropic's Claude API to generate replies. Anthropic acts as our processor and does not use the content to train its models via the API. This may involve a transfer outside the UK (to the United States), protected by appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses.
  • Our hosting provider. The service runs on cloud hosting infrastructure that stores the data on our behalf.

We do not sell your data, and we do not share it with Exchange for Change, ACS, or any other third party for their own marketing.

How long we keep it

If you've consented, we keep your profile and chat for up to 12 months, after which it is automatically deleted. You can delete it sooner at any time. If you decline, nothing is kept.

Your choices and rights

Under UK data protection law you have the right to:

  • See what we hold — use "Privacy & your data → View my data" in the chat.
  • Delete it — use "Delete my data" in the same place. This erases your profile and chat immediately.
  • Withdraw consent at any time, which also deletes what we held.
  • Correct anything inaccurate — just tell the assistant.
  • Object to or restrict our processing.

To exercise any of these, use the in-chat controls or contact us at the address above. You also have the right to complain to the UK regulator, the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) — ico.org.uk — though we'd appreciate the chance to help first.

Automated chat, human decisions

RetEarn Ready uses AI to answer your questions. It does not make any decision that has a legal or similarly significant effect on you. For anything that needs an authoritative answer, please call the expert helpline on +44 7463577990.

Changes to this notice

If we change how we handle your data, we'll update this page and the "last updated" date above.
